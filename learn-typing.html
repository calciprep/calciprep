Where to place your fingers

QWERTY keyboards are standard. Before you start, place your fingers as shown in the image below.

  • Place your left index finger on the 'F' key.
  • Place your right index finger on the 'J' key.
  • Your thumbs should rest lightly on the 'space' bar.

Good luck!
